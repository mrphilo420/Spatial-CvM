1. CHAPTER 2: Estimating the CDF and Statistical Functionals

   - Statistical functionals and plug-in estimators
   - Bootstrap methods

2. CHAPTER 8: Nonparametric Hypothesis Testing  
   - Likely covers goodness-of-fit tests
   - Test statistic construction

   - Relevance to: Theorem2 (CvM null distribution), Calibration

3. CHAPTER 7: Normal Means and Minimax Theory
   - Minimax framework for testing
   - Normal means problem
   - Relevance to: Optimality considerations

RELEVANCE TO OUR AXIOMS:

1. weak_convergence (Theorem1/Main.lean):
   - Wasserman covers Donsker's theorem (empirical process → Brownian bridge)
   - Our axiomatization follows the same mathematical structure
   - Can cite Wasserman Chapter 2 for background

2. continuous_mapping_theorem (Theorem2/Main.lean):
   - Standard result covered in Wasserman
   - Used for deriving CvM distribution from weak convergence

3. delta_method (implied in Theorem2, explicit in Theorem3):
   - Statistical functionals are Hadamard differentiable
   - Fernholz (1983) reference in Wasserman's bibliography is THE key text

4. gaussian_process_exists (Theorem1/Main.lean):
   - Gaussian processes as limits of empirical processes
   - Covered in Wasserman's treatment of Donsker's theorem
